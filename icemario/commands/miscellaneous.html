Miscellaneous Commands

!choose and !teams

These commands can be individually enabled or disabled (disabled by default). They can be enabled by changing the server config settings.

!choose item1, item2, ..., itemN or
!choose num item1, item2, ..., itemN

Randomly choose one or more items in the list of items provided. If num is omitted, Ice Mario will choose 1 item out of the list. Otherwise, Ice Mario wil choose num number of items. The items list can be separated using either a comma or a space.

Examples:

  • !choose banana, orange, mango, strawberry, pineapple: 1 item will be randomly chosen.
  • !choose 1 banana, orange, mango, strawberry, pineapple: 1 item will be randomly chosen.
  • !choose 2 banana orange mango strawberry pineapple: 2 items will be randomly chosen.

!teams # player1, player2, ..., playerN

Randomize n players into the number of teams specified in #.

Examples:

  • !teams 2 player1, player2, ..., player8: Randomly separate 8 players into two teams for a 4v4.
  • !teams 4 player1, player2, ..., player8: Randomly separate 8 players into four teams for a 2v2.

Make Ice Mario Talk

im!say text
Alias: !im

Makes Ice Mario repeat whatever is provided in text.

Here are some example use cases:

  • !im Hello World: A message saying "Hello World" will be sent back.
  • !im :pepeClown:: Ice Mario will send back the pepeClown emoji.


MKT Friend Codes

FCs are taken from Mario Kart Central, and the list updates once a day. Currently, only Mario Kart Tour FCs are supported.

!mktfc playerName1, playerName2, ...

Get the friend codes for one or more MKT players, separated by commas. If Ice Mario is unable to match a name, a list of similar names will be shown, but only if there's not too many.

!mktfct teamName

Get the friend codes for an MKT team. teamName can be a team's full name, their tag, or their acronym.


MKT Ranked Score Calculator

Get an estimate of the possible score on a track given your loadout. Note: these commands were created a few years ago, and there are now much better resources available.

im!score

Get an estimate of the score possible for your loadout. You should type im!score, and Ice Mario will then send a form for you to fill out. Copy the whole message, fill it out, then send it back. For kart rarity, n=normal, s=super, and h=high end.

im!ac

Get an estimate of the action count required to achieve a desired score based on your loadout. This is almost exactly like im!score, but you specify the points instead of Action Count.
